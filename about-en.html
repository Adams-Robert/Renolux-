About Renolux

Your trusted construction partner since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Renolux has become a recognized leader in Quebec's construction industry. Our commitment to excellence, quality, and customer satisfaction has allowed us to build a solid reputation in residential and commercial construction.

With over 15 years of experience, our team of qualified experts has completed hundreds of successful projects, from new construction to complete renovation, including engineering and architecture services.
